High-Intensity Interval Timer (TL;DR)

This is a free, web-based customizable interval timer engineered for High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), circuit workouts, and boxing. It features traffic-light color coding (Prepare, Work, Rest) and runs smoothly in any mobile or desktop browser without app installation. Research shows that structured HIIT protocols can improve VO2 max and anaerobic capacity significantly faster than steady-state cardio.

Popular Training Protocols

  • Tabata: 20s Work / 10s Rest, 8 rounds.
  • Boxing: 3 min Work (180s) / 1 min Rest (60s), 10-12 rounds.
  • EMOM: 60s Work / 0s Rest (perform reps at the start of each minute).

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this fitness timer work offline?

Yes. Once loaded in your browser, it relies entirely on your device's internal clock system. It will continue to track perfectly even if you lose internet connection.

Why use a web timer instead of an app?

Instant access across all devices without draining storage space, requiring permissions, or displaying aggressive full-screen ads.
